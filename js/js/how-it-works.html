How it works

1) Gather

We collect public and private geospatial data: satellite imagery (optical & SAR), drone data, survey data, and open layers (boundaries, terrain, hydrology, demographics).

2) Compose

We overlay layers to answer specific questions, using lightweight web maps or notebooks. Wherever helpful, we use GeoAI (segmentation, change detection, object counting).

3) Validate

We verify with ground truth where available and quantify uncertainty. Results are reproducible and delivered as dashboards, data products, or reports.

4) Ship

We package a simple tool, a repeatable pipeline, or a one-off analysis—whatever best fits the use-case and budget.

Example questions we solve

  • Which facilities are in flood-risk buffers?
  • Where did shoreline or forest cover change since last year?
  • Which ships follow certain corridors, and how has that changed?
  • Where are the best candidate areas for wind or solar (constraints + yield)?
